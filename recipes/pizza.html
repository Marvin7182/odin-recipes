Pizza

Description

The recipe for the fabulous pizza may not be as arduous as you may think.

This lavish meal only requires dough, sauces and herbs, yet it still manages to please connoisseurs.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Olive oil

Procedure

  1. Mix the ingredients listed above
  2. Knead the dough
  3. Put the dough in a greased mixing bowl
  4. Leave for 90 minutes
  5. Punch the risen dough to release air bubbles
  6. Roll out dough into a circle
  7. Add toppings and bake at a high temperature for approximately 15 minutes
